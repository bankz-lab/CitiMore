The Problem

Finding a home in Abuja
will humble you.

The housing market in Nigeria is not broken by accident. It is broken by design — and young people pay the price every single day.

01

Predatory agents everywhere

Fake listings. Inspection fees before you see a room. Agents who take your deposit and show you bad houses. No verification. No accountability. No recourse. You lose the money and start over.

02

Two years rent upfront

Landlords demanding ₦900,000 to ₦2000,500,000 in cash before you sign anything. Young Nigerians breaking savings, borrowing from family, settling for unsafe areas because decent housing was priced out of reach.

03

No safe way to share

Splitting rent solves everything — but who do you trust? WhatsApp groups full of strangers. No verification, no compatibility check, no ratings. Moving in with the wrong person becomes its own nightmare.

The Solution

Simple enough to use
in 60 seconds.

CitiMore works in four steps. No middlemen. No stress. Just safe, verified housing.
